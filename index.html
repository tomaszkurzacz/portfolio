About

My name is Tomasz Kurzacz and I have been professionally dealing with post-production of product photos for over 5 years, which allowed me to gain extensive experience in this specialization.

In my work, attention to quality and accuracy is a priority for me. That is why I always put a lot of effort and heart into every task entrusted to me, which translates into its high quality and satisfaction with the final result. Each new challenge is an opportunity for me to develop and expand my skills, which is why I always try to approach it with full commitment and determination to get the best result.

In addition to exploring the secrets of graphics, I also develop my skills in the field of 3D modeling in Blender. It is an additional tool for me to create original projects and make my works more attractive.

In addition to professional interests, in my free time I like to do sports, enjoy good food and read books. I am always hungry for new experiences and eager to learn, so I am constantly trying to learn new things and develop my skills.
